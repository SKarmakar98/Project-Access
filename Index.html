What is Project Access?

We're a non-profit organisation creating a world where passion and potential define a young person's future.
We've created a program to support underrepresented, high potential students apply to undergraduate and graduate programs at top UK, US and EU universities.
For students, by students, and forever free-of-charge.
At the core of our programs is one-on-one mentorship, matching high school students with a
mentor who studies their dream degree at their dream university. Alongside, our mentees will
join a community of applicants from around the world and get access to the best resources
and webinars out there.
